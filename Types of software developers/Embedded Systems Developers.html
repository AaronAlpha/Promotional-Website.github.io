Job Description:

helps design, develop, and maintain embedded systems in products. The role often includes software development. But it also requires knowledge of entire embedded systems. There isn't a concrete definition of the role of an embedded engineer because it can vary by organization. An embedded engineer will work on embedded systems that need to accomplish a specific task. The engineer will often develop the software that's part of the system. The engineer may also help test the entire system to ensure it works well.



Salary:

$94,750 avg per yr according to Glassdoor in Edmonton, Alberta



Requirement/Prequisites for job:

require extensive knowledge of different aspects of engineering, software and technology. Purse the a relevant degree of education; gain experience through training; Choose an industry to specialize in; Consider obtaining certifications
